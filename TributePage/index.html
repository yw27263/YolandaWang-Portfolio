About

Born on August 31, 2025, in the depths of an undisclosed washing machine cycle, he emerged with unmatched charisma, unbrushed teeth, and eyes that have seen too much. Within days, he captured hearts worldwide and was declared a World Wide Icon™ by the International Federation of Fabric Beings.
